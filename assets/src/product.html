Q: What is "quick disconnect" on Jabra Headsets?

A: A quick disconnect is a connector typically found on headsets that allows the user to detach the headset from the device it's connected to, without having to remove the connector from the headset. This can be useful for times when you need to step away from your desk, but don't want to have to fuss with unplugging your headphones. There are a few different types of quick disconnects, but they all work in basically the same way. The connector has a small tab that locks into place when it's inserted into the jack, and you release the tab to disconnect it. Some connectors have a rotating sleeve that you twist to lock/unlock it, while others have a button you press.
